"The Innovator's Dilemma" Book Notes

  • reads like a textbook
  • too much info about disk drives
  • disruptive technologies work like this: development-> companies -> markets -> users -> upmarket -> established firms defend their sustaining tech
  • iPhone 2G=disruptive, iPhone 8=sustaining
  • If a company’s resources, processes and values don’t match the market, no management can save it.
  • Market leaders can solve the innovator’s dilemma by acquiring or founding subsidiaries.
  • smaller companies shoot for smaller goals to achieve the same goals
