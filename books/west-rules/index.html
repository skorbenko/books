"Why the West Rules" Impression

please note: this impression is an excerpt from a letter to my friend.

As for "Why the West Rules", my opinion has stayed largely the same, but I found myself realizing something important. I always wondered if, just maybe, the democratic notion I was taught in London- that all people are equal- is wrong. This book finally proved to me, scientifically, that there are no reasons for racism whatsoever, and racists are mostly uncultured humans who don't read many books or scientific articles/papers.
